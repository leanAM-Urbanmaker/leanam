2. Hosting — Cloudflare Pages

This website is hosted via Cloudflare Pages (Cloudflare, Inc., 101 Townsend St., San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). When the website is accessed, technical connection data (IP address, browser, operating system, date/time) is automatically recorded in server log files.

Cloudflare processes this data on the basis of appropriate safeguards pursuant to Art. 46 GDPR (EU Standard Contractual Clauses). Cloudflare is also certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (https://www.dataprivacyframework.gov/), which recognizes an adequate level of data protection for transfers to the USA. For further information, please refer to Cloudflare's privacy policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in the reliable and secure provision of the website).

To send our contact form, we use the service Web3Forms, operated by:

Spacedev (Web3Forms)
Aditya Dhanraj
India
Web: https://web3forms.com

When you submit the contact form, the data you enter (name, email address, message and, where applicable, further voluntary information) is transmitted to Web3Forms for processing and forwarding to our email address hq@leanAM.com. In doing so, the data is transferred to a third country (India) for which no adequacy decision by the European Commission exists.

The legal basis for the processing is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (initiation and performance of a contractual relationship) as well as Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (our legitimate interest in efficient handling of inquiries). We base the transfer to the third country on Art. 49(1)(b) GDPR (necessity for the performance of pre-contractual measures taken at the request of the data subject). Insofar as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC, Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR) have additionally been concluded with the provider, these also apply.

The data is deleted as soon as it is no longer required for processing the inquiry, but at the latest upon expiry of statutory retention periods.

4. Appointment booking — Calendly

For booking demo appointments and initial consultations, we link to the service Calendly, operated by:

Calendly LLC
271 17th St NW, Suite 1000
Atlanta, GA 30363, USA
Privacy: https://calendly.com/privacy

Calendly is not embedded on our website — no data is transmitted to Calendly when you merely visit our pages. Only when you click a booking link do you leave our website and reach Calendly's booking page. There, Calendly processes the data you enter (name, email address, chosen appointment, and any voluntary information) as well as technical connection data on servers in the USA.

The legal basis for the processing is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of pre-contractual measures at your request — arranging an appointment). The transfer to the USA is based on the adequacy decision for the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, under which Calendly is certified (https://www.dataprivacyframework.gov/), and additionally on EU Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR).

Booking an appointment via Calendly is voluntary — alternatively, you can arrange appointments with us at any time by email or telephone.

This website does not use any marketing or tracking cookies. No third-party analytics or advertising services (such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel or similar) are used. Technically necessary cookies may be set by Cloudflare Pages as part of the hosting.

This website loads fonts (Inter, JetBrains Mono) from its own servers — no connection is made to external font provider servers (e.g. the Google Fonts CDN).
