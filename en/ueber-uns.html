Mission · Origin · Values

We know AM — because we run it ourselves, every day.

leanAM started on the production floor. What we recommend, we have built ourselves, and what we develop runs through our own machines every day.

Why leanAM

No hype, no empty promises. leanAM – your partner for measurable value with additive manufacturing in your processes.

For years, manufacturers and consultants have promised that AM will revolutionize production. The reality in many plants looks different: isolated machines, missing software, hardly any integration into the value stream.

We founded leanAM to change that. We bring proper industrial tooling, advice that tells you the truth, and software that keeps running once the demo is over.

We see AM as an industrial technology. It needs industrial processes, industrial tooling and partners who run it themselves every day.

Behind leanAM

leanAM is a brand of URBANMAKER.

URBANMAKER is a German company with deep roots in manufacturing, technology and applied engineering. leanAM grew out of it as a focused brand for software, consulting and development around additive manufacturing.

So behind leanAM sits a company that actually runs a production operation, knows its technology and wants to ship solutions that survive everyday industrial use.
