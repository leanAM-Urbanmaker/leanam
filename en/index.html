// OUR PROCESS

From the first analysis to a scaling AM line.

Four phases, each with defined deliverables, from the first conversation to series production. You always know where the project stands.

T0 · Kickoff T+ weeks →
  1. PHASE_01 1–2 weeks

    Analysis

    We review your part portfolio, your volumes and requirements, then identify the use cases with the strongest business case.

    • Part screening
    • Use-case matrix
    • Business case
  2. PHASE_02 3–6 weeks

    Concept

    We pick the right technology, materials and machines, then design the process layout, IT architecture and business case.

    • Technology & material selection
    • Process & IT layout
    • Investment planning
  3. PHASE_03 8–16 weeks

    Implementation

    We commission the hardware, roll out the leanAM software, hook it into ERP/CAQ and train your team on site.

    • Hardware commissioning
    • Software & ERP/CAQ
    • Team enablement
  4. PHASE_04 ongoing

    Scaling

    We stay on through ramp-up, tune the KPIs and, if you want, take on peak loads as a B2B service.

    • KPI tuning
    • Ramp-up support
    • B2B overflow optional
